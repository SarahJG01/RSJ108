How to create a successful blog

Review time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

As media professionals, the first step to getting our message out is capturing the audiences attention. With the continous growth of internet use, a blog is a great way to reach the audience.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Your computer
  • Internet connection
  • Patience

  1. Don't blog just to blog

    When creating a blog brainstorm a reason to blog. Choose a topic that you and the audience will find intersting, and maintain an interest in. Make sure that you can maintain a steady stream of content over time.

  2. Establish a tone

    Establishing a tone, will create the overall feeling of your blog. The tone assists with getting your message across. This is an important step, so make sure you take time brainstorming what you want your tone to exude. For further information regarding tone, watch the provided video.

  3. Provide quick reads and be timely

    Using short bits of information, you can provide the most important info and then move on. This information should be immediately useful to readers, by informing them of something that just happened.

  4. Showcase additional content

    Adding additional links into blog posts suggests additional content to your readers, and can draw them deeper into your site.

  5. Don't just react

    Before you post make sure you reflect on the situation, why it is important to discuss, and what you can bring to the discussion as a whole.

  6. Update your audience

    Keep your digital work updated with current and relevant content as situations unfold. This will grow your audience and keep readers interested.
